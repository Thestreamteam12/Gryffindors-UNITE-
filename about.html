The Very Beginning

Hogwarts was founded by four wizards and witches: Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin. It became a boarding school for many other wizards and witches willing to learn the many ways or magic. The school is over a thousand years old, and it is located a few hours from London, England. These four founders represented the personality traits of the houses that students got assigned to. There was, however, a quarrel concerning if muggles or half-bloods could attend the school. Salazar was set on keeping the school for pure-bloods, while the others thought otherwise. Before he left the school, he eventually built the Chamber of Secrets, where only his own heir would be able to gain entry and unleash a basilisk, or the Serpent of Slytherin, to get rid of all muggle-born and half-blood students. Naturally, Godric and Salazar were friends no longer, as Godric believed that all students - including muggles and half-bloods - should be given a chance to learn magic if they were capable of learning.
