The Traits of the Houses





Gryffindor's Traits

The Gryffindor house emphasises the traits of courage as well as daring, nerve, and chivalry, and thus its members are generally regarded as brave, though sometimes to the point of recklessness. They can also be short-tempered, and even do things without thinking. Because of these traits, their symbol is the lion, which naturally represents all the traist stated for a Gryffindor.









Slytherin's Traits

The Slytherin house is well known for being quite clever and resourceful, along with their amazing abilties to be leaders. Some members of the house may be a bit bossy because of this, but they are dependable when you need them. They will never leave their own behind, so they have quite an efficient way of teamwork.








Ravenclaw Traits

Members of this house are characterised by their wit, learning, and wisdom. Ravenclaw corresponds roughly to the element of air. Ravenclaws possess the traits of cleverness, wisdom, wit, and creativity. Many well-known inventors and innovators have come from Ravenclaw. They tend to accept others better, unlike other classes, and have a sense of individuality.








HufflePuff Traits

Hufflepuff is the most inclusive among the four houses; valuing hard work, dedication, patience, loyalty, and fair play. They're well-known to be modest and introverted, and they're not as comptitive in their daily lives as other classes. In the quidditch court, however, be prepared to face a competitive game.
